📘 How to Use the HSSE Web App

1. Dashboard Overview

The homepage displays national HSSE metrics such as incidents by type, most fined companies, and trends over time.

2. Map Visualization

Click on the Data Map tab to view incidents geographically. Hover over markers to see incident details and severity levels.

3. Voice Assistant

The Assistant tab lets you ask questions using your voice. Click the 🎤 Ask button, speak your question, and wait for a response.

  • Examples: “Tell me about the data.” or “Who was fined the most?”
  • Click ⛔ Stop to cancel ongoing audio or speech synthesis.

4. Reporting an Incident

Use the report form to submit a new incident with details, image upload, and location. The image is scanned for PPE violations using AI.

  • Fill out the description, choose a severity level, and upload an image.
  • The AI will detect if safety gear like helmets or vests are missing.

5. Exporting Chat or Reports

Use the export buttons in the assistant or dashboard to download your data or chat history as .txt or .pdf.

Need help? Contact your administrator or refer to the portal guide.
